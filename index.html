You probably want to know a little about me, so here we go:

Ever since I was 9, I wanted to be around technology. I grew up and technology surrounds me! Now, I am amused by the logic behind technology and how everything operates! So web development places me to my field of interest and I feel like I found my place in life!

On top of all of this, it is the closest thing that I can get to magic so web development is my magic!
